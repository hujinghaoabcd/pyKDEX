# Roadmap

1. fixed spatial KDE and engineering baseline;
2. bandwidth selectors and adaptive spatial KDE;
3. separable and cyclic spatiotemporal KDE;
4. network topology, snapping, lixels, and shortest-path support;
5. simple, discontinuous, continuous, and heat-kernel NKDE;
6. product-kernel and adaptive temporal network KDE;
7. exposure-adjusted risk, uncertainty, and separability diagnostics;
8. scalable and streaming research estimators.
